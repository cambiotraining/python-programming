#### Help Session

Please feel free to reach out to the trainers for any support you need to get started with Python. This may include assistance with installing Python itself, setting up environments such as Conda, or installing any other required packages and tools.

We are also available to help you work through the exercises, clarify any concepts that are unclear, and guide you toward additional learning resources that may be useful for your progress.
:::
